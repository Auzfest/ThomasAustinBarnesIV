DreamBox

Due to confedentiality reasons this project is not available to the public yet.

The Web Development and Design Society at BYU-I has taken the task to remake DreamBox's Wix website. A local selfie studio that offers a variety of backdrops and props for photo shoots. We are recreating the website using Next.js, TypeScript and Tailwind to provide a better experience for the users and owners. This project is still in progress but feel free to see their Wix site for an idea of what it looks like.

Soccer Statistics Website

A continuing passion project showing soccer statistics for professional soccer leagues across the world. Using Football_API anyone can find any league, any team and any player to see their statistics of the latest seasons with this website. This project is still in progress. With a backend database in the works to make accounts and easily see your favorite leagues, teams and players.
